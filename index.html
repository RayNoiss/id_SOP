About We Environment

Welcome to Save Our Planet! We are a community dedicated to raising awareness
about the importance of keeping our environment clean.
We believe that every small step towards a cleaner environment has a huge impact on the health and well-being of us all.
Our Mission
Our mission is to provide comprehensive education on how to keep the environment
clean through easy-to-understand content that can be applied by everyone, from young children to adults.
Our Vision
We envision a world where every individual is aware of their responsibility towards the environment.
With small steps taken collectively, we can create a cleaner and greener environment for future generations.

Medan, Bandar Lampung, and Manado have been named as the dirtiest cities in Indonesia as assessed by the Ministry of Environment and Forestry (KLHK) in the Adipura program.

1. Ineffective Waste Management: These cities have major problems with waste management.
A lot of waste is not managed properly and is often dumped openly.
2. Quality of Landfills: The value of landfills in these cities is very low.
A poor landfill means that waste is not processed in a safe and sanitary way.
3. Lack of Commitment and Budget: Local governments often lack commitment and budget to address waste issues.
This leads to a lack of public participation and efforts to improve the situation.
4. Physical Condition of the City: The physical condition of the city, including the cleanliness of streets and public areas, is also very poor.
This reflects the lack of adequate waste services
